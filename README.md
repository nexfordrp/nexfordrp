## 🏛️ Government System

Nexford City menjalankan sistem pemerintahan demokratis penuh in-game:

```
Governor (elected)
├── Police Chief        — Nexford Police Department
├── EMS Director        — Nexford Emergency Services
├── Fire Chief          — Nexford Fire Department
├── City Council        — Legislative body (3–5 seats)
├── Tax Collector       — City treasury management
└── DA / Prosecutor     — Nexford Courthouse
```
